About

Dylan Crowe is a singer-songwriter touring Australia from the seat of a van, with a guitar in the back and a dog named Maverick riding shotgun. His performances span the spectrum from delicate acoustic ballads to high-energy rock covers — Beatles to Foo Fighters, played raw on a street corner or an intimate stage.

Living on the road, Dylan has built a following one town at a time. He doesn't wait for an audience — he finds one. Every busking session is a show. Every street corner is a venue.

Currently on a national tour from the Sunshine Coast to Perth and back — crossing the Nullarbor twice, busking every major town, playing venues along the way — Dylan is a self-contained solo act who shows up, plugs in, and plays. No fuss. No ego. Just music.
