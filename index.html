Touring Australia

About

Dylan Crowe is a singer-songwriter touring Australia from the seat of a van, with a guitar in the back and a dog named Maverick riding shotgun. His performances span the spectrum from delicate acoustic ballads to high-energy rock covers — Beatles to Foo Fighters, played raw on a street corner or an intimate stage.

Living on the road, Dylan has built a following one town at a time. He doesn't wait for an audience — he finds one. Every busking session is a show. Every street corner is a venue.

Currently on a national tour from the Sunshine Coast to Perth and back — crossing the Nullarbor twice, busking every major town, playing venues along the way — Dylan is a self-contained solo act who shows up, plugs in, and plays. No fuss. No ego. Just music.

Bio Blurbs

Short — for event listings
Dylan Crowe is a singer-songwriter touring Australia with his guitar and dog Maverick. From delicate acoustic ballads to high-energy Foo Fighters covers — a self-contained solo act who shows up and plays.
Medium — for website features
Dylan Crowe brings raw, road-worn music to every corner of Australia. Touring from a van with his dog Maverick by his side, Dylan delivers everything from tender Beatles covers to explosive Foo Fighters anthems — all on a single acoustic guitar. Self-contained, no-fuss, and road-tested on street corners from the Sunshine Coast to Perth. One guitar. One dog. Every town.
Long — for press & media
Dylan Crowe is a singer-songwriter living the Australian dream — touring the country from the seat of a van, with a guitar in the back and a border collie named Maverick riding shotgun. His music spans the spectrum from delicate acoustic ballads to full-throated rock covers, drawing from a deep well of influences including The Beatles, Foo Fighters, and classic Australian pub rock. Currently on a national tour crossing the Nullarbor twice, Dylan has built a following the old-fashioned way — one street corner, one busking session, one town at a time. He's a self-contained solo act who needs ten minutes to set up and can play anywhere from 45 minutes to two hours. No fuss. No ego. Just music.
