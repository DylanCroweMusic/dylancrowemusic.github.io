DYLAN CROWE — TERMS & CONDITIONS
=================================

Last updated: June 2026


1. BOOKING & CONFIRMATION

1.1  A booking is confirmed when both parties agree on date, time, fee, and
     performance duration in writing (email or message).

1.2  The Artist will provide a Booking Confirmation via email within 48 hours
     of verbal/written agreement. The Venue should review and confirm receipt.


2. PERFORMANCE

2.1  Act type: Solo acoustic — guitar and vocals. Self-contained with own
     amplification where required.

2.2  Standard set lengths: 2 × 45 min, 3 × 40 min, or as agreed. The Artist
     can perform up to 4 hours.

2.3  Setup time: 10–15 minutes. The Artist requires no load-in assistance but
     asks that the performance area be clear and accessible on arrival.

2.4  Set break: 10–20 minutes between sets, at the Artist's discretion.

2.5  The Venue must provide:
       - A safe, weather-protected performance space with access to power
       - One chair or stool
       - One complimentary meal and non-alcoholic drink (or equivalent
         allowance) for bookings of 2+ hours

2.6  Outdoor performances: The Artist reserves the right to pause or cancel
     if weather conditions threaten safety or equipment (heavy rain, extreme
     heat, electrical storms).


3. FEES & PAYMENT

3.1  Performance fee is agreed in writing at time of booking.

3.2  Payment is due in full immediately after the performance, unless
     alternative terms are agreed in writing.

3.3  For new venue relationships or bookings above AUD $500, a 25% deposit
     may be requested and is payable within 7 days of Booking Confirmation.

3.4  Payment methods: Cash, bank transfer, or as agreed.

3.5  Late payment: Invoices unpaid after 14 days may incur a late fee of
     AUD $25 plus 5% of the outstanding amount per month.


4. CANCELLATION

4.1  Cancellation by Venue:
       - 14+ days before the date: no charge
       - 7–13 days before the date: 50% of the agreed fee
       - Less than 7 days before the date: 100% of the agreed fee
     The deposit (if paid) is non-refundable for cancellations within 7 days.

4.2  Cancellation by Artist:
     The Artist will make every effort to honour all confirmed bookings. In the
     unlikely event the Artist must cancel (illness, emergency, or circumstances
     beyond reasonable control), the Artist will:
       - Notify the Venue as soon as reasonably possible
       - Refund any deposit paid in full
       - Offer to reschedule at a mutually agreeable date
     The Artist's liability is limited to the deposit refund.


5. VENUE OBLIGATIONS

5.1  The Venue is responsible for all necessary licences, permits, and
     regulatory compliance (liquor licence, noise curfews, performance
     rights if applicable).

5.2  The Venue must ensure the performance area is safe, clear of hazards,
     and compliant with workplace health and safety standards.

5.3  The Venue grants the Artist permission to sell personal merchandise
     (if applicable) and to display a tip jar or QR code for audience tips.

5.4  The Artist may photograph, film, or livestream their own performance
     for personal and promotional use.


6. FORCE MAJEURE

6.1  Neither party is liable for failure to perform obligations caused by
     events beyond reasonable control, including but not limited to: natural
     disasters, severe weather, fire, government restrictions, pandemic
     lockdowns, or transport failure.

6.2  The affected party must notify the other as soon as reasonably possible.

6.3  If the event prevents the performance from proceeding, the booking is
     cancelled without penalty and any deposit paid is refunded in full.
