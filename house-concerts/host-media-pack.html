💡 Quick Tips

Send the link early. Early bird tickets ($20) go fast and create urgency. Share the link as soon as you have it.

Post twice. First post: "I'm doing this!" Second post (3 days before): "Only X spots left!" Creates FOMO.

Tag Dylan. @dylan_crowe_music on Instagram — he'll reshare and that brings more eyes to your event.

Tell people to bring friends. "+1s welcome" doubles your reach with zero effort.

It's BYO. Remind people — they bring drinks and a plate. You don't cater. You host the vibe, not the bar.
