Who's Playing?

Dylan Crowe is a singer-songwriter touring Australia with a guitar, a van, and his dog Maverick. He's played street corners from the Sunshine Coast to Perth, pub stages in every state, and now — living rooms.

His sets span tender Beatles ballads to explosive Foo Fighters covers, all stripped down to one voice and one guitar. No backing tracks. No safety net. Just the kind of performance that hits different when you're ten feet away on a couch.
