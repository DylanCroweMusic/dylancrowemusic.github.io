FAQ

Probably wondering...

What kind of space do I need?

A living room, open-plan area, covered deck, or backyard. Enough room for people to sit comfortably and see Dylan perform. No stage or lighting needed — this is intimate, not a stadium.

What about noise / neighbours?

It's one guy with an acoustic guitar — not a full band. The volume is conversation-level. If your neighbours are cool with a dinner party, they'll be cool with this.

Do I need to provide food and drinks?

Nope — guests bring their own drinks and a plate to share (potluck style). You just provide the space and the vibe. Some hosts choose to provide basics but there's zero obligation.

What about the dog?

Maverick is Dylan's Australian Shepherd — he travels everywhere with Dylan. He's calm, friendly, and loves pats. If anyone at your event isn't comfortable with dogs, just let Dylan know beforehand.

How do tickets work?

Dylan sets up the event on Humanitix (Aussie ticketing, low fees). You get a custom invitation link to share with your friends. They buy tickets online. You + partner get free promo codes. Simple.

How far in advance should I book?

2–4 weeks is ideal — gives your friends time to lock in the date. But Dylan's on the road full-time and sometimes has gaps — if you're flexible, reach out even on short notice.
