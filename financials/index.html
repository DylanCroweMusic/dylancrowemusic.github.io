Why house concerts on weekdays? Hosts don't care what night it is — their friends show up regardless. But venues only book Thu–Sun. Put house concerts on Mon/Tue/Wed and you never compete with your own gig nights. It's the scheduling unlock that makes 7+ income events per week sustainable.
