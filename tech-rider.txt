DYLAN CROWE — TECH RIDER
=========================

ACT TYPE: Solo Acoustic (guitar & vocals)

SETUP REQUIREMENTS:
  - One stool or armless chair
  - One power outlet within 3m of performance area
  - Performance area: minimum 2m × 2m
  - Setup time: 10 minutes
  - Pack-down time: 5 minutes

GEAR (SELF-PROVIDED):
  - Acoustic-electric guitar (Maton)
  - LR Baggs Venue DI / preamp
  - Vocal microphone (Shure SM58)
  - Mic stand
  - Instrument cables (3m)
  - Small pedalboard (tuner, reverb, looper optional)

PA REQUIREMENTS:
  Option A: Dylan is fully self-contained with a portable
  battery-powered PA system (suitable for venues up to ~100
  capacity or outdoor busking-style setups).

  Option B: House PA (preferred)
    - 1× XLR input for vocal mic
    - 1× 1/4" instrument input (or DI box provided by venue)
    - 1× foldback monitor preferred but not essential

LIGHTING:
  Basic front wash sufficient. No special lighting required.

STAGE PLOT:
  Simple — one performer, centre stage, seated on stool with
  guitar. Mic stand to performer's left/right depending on
  dominant hand.

HOSPITALITY:
  Water (still or sparkling).
  A meal if a dinner venue — anything, no dietary restrictions.
